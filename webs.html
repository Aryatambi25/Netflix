Social media can be a useful source of market information and a way to hear customer perspectives. Blogs, content communities, and forums are platforms where individuals share their reviews and recommendations of brands, products, and services.

Social media

According to Constantinides, Lorenzo and Gómez Borja (2008) "Business executives like Jonathan Swartz, President and CEO of Sun Microsystems, Steve Jobs CEO of Apple Computers, and McDonald's Vice President Bob Langert post regularly in their CEO blogs, encouraging customers to interact and freely express their feelings, ideas, suggestions, or remarks about their postings, the company or its products".

Rise of Short-Form & Authentic
Video Content

Videos under 60 seconds (like Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, or TikToks)
are trending because they’re quick, engaging, and easy to watch.
People scroll fast, so short videos grab attention instantly.

AI-Powered Personalization &
Content Creation

AI studies user behavior — what you like, watch, comment on —
and then shows you content, ads, and recommendations that match your interests.
Example: Instagram suggesting reels you’d enjoy or
YouTube recommending videos based on your watch history.

Social Commerce & In-App
Shopping Boom

This is when shopping happens inside social platforms like Instagram, Facebook,
TikTok, or YouTube — where users can browse, view details,
and purchase without leaving the app.

Community-Driven & Value
-Based Engagement

Users now prefer smaller, meaningful groups or creators
who make them feel part of a community — like fandoms,
interest-based groups, or brand-led communities.
